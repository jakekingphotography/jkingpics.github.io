Who I Am

I am a Photographer. I take photos of things.

Welcome to my website. My name is Jacob King. I am almost 14 years old and picked up photography as a hobby about a year ago (2018 or something like that). Every single picture on my website has been taken in the same neighborhood since I bought my camera. The idea for making a proper website for my photos was my brother’s idea and he is taking time out of his day to help me make it happen. He’s in college. Probably really busy. Not sure what he’s thinking. But enough about him. This is my website after all. Even though he's doing most of (if not all of) the work. I just gave him pictures and let him do his thing. Incase you didnt know he’s the, “website master.” don't hate on my pictures too hard. thanks.
